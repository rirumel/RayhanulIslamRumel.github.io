Breast Cancer Detection using Python

I used the Wisconsin Diagnostic Breast Cancer (WDBC) dataset to create the predictive models that can classify benign and malignant tissue. Here, I used two classifiers: Decision Tree, and K-nearest neighbor. I calculated the measurement matrices like Accuracy, Precison and Recall of both of the models to compare which model performed the best to solve the problem.
